Keesaflo by Keesaco

The cloud based, colaborative flow cytometry tool
Year project in the Team Keesaco in colaboration with Potato (Bristol)

October 2013 to June 2014 - Web Development - Keesaco @ UOB

Flow cytometry is a technology that is used to analyse the physical and chemical characteristics of particles in a fluid as it passes through at least one laser. Cell components are fluorescently labelled and then excited by the laser to emit light at varying wavelengths.

Dr Ananya Mandal, MD in News-Medical.net

Applications are:

  • Cell counting
  • Cell sorting
  • Detection of biomarkers
  • Protein engineering

Our Team (Keesaco) worked on developing a web aplication on google app engine (to make it scalable) allowing one to manage and process flowcytometry datasets.
I was mainly in charge of the front end interface, including javascript, html5, css3 and other JS libraries.
